## Quick Reference

### HLVd Prevention Checklist
1. PCR test all incoming genetics before introducing to facility
2. Quarantine new plants for 2 weeks minimum
3. Sterilize cutting tools between every plant (10% bleach or isopropyl)
4. Test mother plants on 90-day cycles
5. Remove and destroy any confirmed positive plants immediately
6. Do not reuse growing media from HLVd-positive plants

### Clone Rooting Quick Guide
1. Take 4-6 inch cuts from vegetative mothers (low N for 5-7 days prior)
2. Cut at 45° below node, immediately place in pH 5.8 water
3. Remove lower leaves, cut remaining fans in half
4. Dip in rooting gel, insert into pre-soaked rockwool (pH 5.5-5.8)
5. Dome at 95%+ humidity, 75-80°F, 100-150 PPFD
6. Do NOT water cubes for first 4 days
7. Crack vents gradually days 5-7
8. Roots visible days 7-10, transplant days 10-14
