# Cannabis Clone Buying FAQ

## For First-Time Buyers

### What is a cannabis clone?
A clone is a genetically identical cutting taken from a mother plant. It produces the same genetics as the parent — same cannabinoid profile, same terpene expression, same growth characteristics.

### Why buy clones instead of seeds?
- **Guaranteed sex**: Every clone is female (no wasted time on males)
- **Genetic consistency**: Same results every run, unlike seed variation
- **Faster to harvest**: Skip 2-3 weeks of seedling stage
- **Known performance**: You know exactly what the strain produces

### What should I look for when buying clones?
1. **HLVd testing** — Ask if mother plants are PCR tested. This is the #1 quality indicator.
2. **Root development** — Healthy white roots visible at the bottom of the cube
3. **Vigor** — New growth emerging, green healthy leaves
4. **No pests** — Check undersides of leaves with magnification

### What is HLVd and why should I care?
Hop Latent Viroid is a pathogen that reduces yields 30-50% with no cure. 30-40% of commercial clones carry it. Only PCR testing can detect it. [Full HLVd info](https://www.primecutsnursery.com/hplvd-testing)

### How do I transport clones safely?
- Keep them upright
- Maintain humidity (plastic dome or bag with holes)
- Avoid temperature extremes (keep 65-80°F)
- Transplant within 24-48 hours of purchase
- Do not leave in a hot car

## Choosing the Right Genetics

### How do I pick the right strain?
Consider your growing environment:
- **Short ceiling / small tent**: OG Kush family, Wedding Cake, GMO (compact growth)
- **Tall space**: Blue Dream, Jack Herer, Sativa-dominant strains
- **Hot climate**: Afghan/Kush genetics (heat tolerant)
- **Humid climate**: Mold-resistant strains (Durban Poison, Jack Herer)

### What about autoflower clones?
Autoflowers cannot be cloned effectively. Their internal clock means clones flower immediately at whatever size they are. Seeds only for autoflowers.

## After Purchase

### How do I transplant my clones?
1. Pre-water your final container with pH 6.0-6.5 nutrient solution (1/4 strength)
2. Dig a hole slightly larger than the rockwool cube
3. Place clone in hole, backfill gently
4. Water around (not directly on) the stem
5. Keep humidity high for first 2-3 days (dome optional)
6. Gradual light increase over first week

### When should I flip to flower?
- Fill 60-70% of your canopy area before flipping
- Most indicas double in height during flower (2x stretch)
- Most sativas can triple (3x stretch)
- Account for stretch when deciding flip height

### How many clones do I need for a 4x4 tent?
- SOG (Sea of Green): 16-25 small plants
- Moderate training: 4-9 plants with topping/LST
- Big plants: 1-4 with heavy training
