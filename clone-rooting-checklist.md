# Cannabis Clone Rooting Checklist

## Pre-Cut Preparation (Day -7 to Day 0)

- [ ] Reduce nitrogen on mother plants 5-7 days before cutting
- [ ] Sterilize all tools (10% bleach, 30 sec soak)
- [ ] Pre-soak rockwool cubes in pH 5.5-5.8 water
- [ ] Prepare humidity dome (clean, sanitized)
- [ ] Set up T5/LED at 100-150 PPFD
- [ ] Prepare rooting gel (Clonex or equivalent)
- [ ] Set up heat mat (target 77-80°F at media level)
- [ ] Fill spray bottle with pH 5.8 water

## Taking Cuts (Day 0)

- [ ] Select 4-6 inch branch tips with 2-3 nodes
- [ ] Cut at 45° angle just below a node
- [ ] Immediately place cut end in pH 5.8 water
- [ ] Remove lower leaves (keep top 2-3)
- [ ] Cut remaining fan leaves in half
- [ ] Optional: lightly score stem 1 inch up from cut
- [ ] Dip 1-1.5 inches in rooting gel (even coat)
- [ ] Insert into pre-moistened rockwool cube
- [ ] Place in humidity dome, close all vents

## Days 1-4: High Humidity Phase

- [ ] Dome humidity: 95-100%
- [ ] Temperature at media: 75-80°F
- [ ] Light: 18/6 at 100-150 PPFD
- [ ] Mist dome lid and leaves 2x daily
- [ ] **DO NOT water cubes** — initial soak is enough
- [ ] Remove any dead/yellow leaf material
- [ ] Check for mold daily

## Days 5-7: Transition Phase

- [ ] Day 5: Open vents 25%
- [ ] Day 6: Open vents 50%
- [ ] Day 7: Open vents 75%
- [ ] Stop misting leaves
- [ ] If leaves wilt badly, close vents and try again next day
- [ ] Monitor for root tips emerging from cubes

## Days 7-14: Root Development

- [ ] Roots visible? Remove dome entirely
- [ ] Bottom-water with 1/4 strength nutrients (EC 0.4-0.6)
- [ ] Increase light gradually to 200-300 PPFD
- [ ] Maintain 65-70% ambient humidity
- [ ] Check all clones are standing upright
- [ ] New growth visible at top = ready for transplant

## Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Likely Cause | Fix |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| Yellowing leaves (day 3-5) | Normal energy redirect | Wait — not concerning |
| Brown/mushy stem base | Overwatering | Discard, reduce water |
| Wilting that won't recover | Air embolism during cut | Discard, re-cut new clones |
| No roots at day 14 | Cold root zone or slow genetics | Check temp, wait until day 21 |
| Mold on leaves | Too humid, poor air flow | Increase ventilation |

## Rooting Speed by Genetics (General Ranges)

**Fast (6-8 days):** OG Kush, Gelato, Wedding Cake, GMO
**Medium (8-10 days):** Blue Dream, GG4, Runtz, GSC
**Slow (10-14 days):** Jack Herer, Durban Poison, Haze varieties

## Critical Reminders

1. **Never let the cut end dry** before placing in media
2. **Never overwater clones** — #1 killer
3. **Always sterilize between plants** — prevents HLVd transmission
4. **Test mother plants** — no point rooting infected genetics
