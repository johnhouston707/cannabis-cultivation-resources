# Organic Cannabis Growing Inputs Reference

## Approved Organic Inputs for Cannabis Cultivation

This reference covers common organic amendments, fertilizers, and pest management products used in clean cannabis production.

## Soil Amendments

### Base Mix Components

| Input | NPK Ratio | Application Rate | Purpose |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|---------|
| Worm castings | 1-0-0 | 20-30% of mix | Microbiology, gentle N |
| Pumice | 0-0-0 | 20-30% of mix | Drainage, aeration |
| Coco coir | 0-0-0 | 10-20% of mix | Water retention |
| Compost | Varies | 15-25% of mix | Nutrition base |
| Biochar | 0-0-0 | 5-10% of mix | CEC, microbial housing |

### Mineral Amendments

| Input | Purpose | Application |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| Glacial rock dust | Broad mineral spectrum | 1-2 cups per cu ft |
| Gypsum (CaSO4) | Calcium + sulfur | 1/2 cup per cu ft |
| Oyster shell flour | Slow-release calcium, pH buffer | 1/2 cup per cu ft |
| Langbeinite | K-Mg-S (0-0-22) | 1/4 cup per cu ft |
| Soft rock phosphate | Slow P release | 1/2 cup per cu ft |

## Organic Fertilizers (Top-Dress & Tea)

### Nitrogen Sources
- Neem seed meal (6-1-2) — also has pest-repellent properties
- Alfalfa meal (3-1-2) — contains triacontanol growth stimulant
- Fish meal (10-6-0) — fast-acting N
- Crustacean meal (4-0-0) — chitin triggers plant immune response

### Phosphorus Sources
- Bone meal (3-15-0) — classic P source
- Seabird guano (1-10-1) — fast-acting flower P
- Fish bone meal (4-12-0) — gentler than raw bone meal

### Potassium Sources
- Kelp meal (1-0-2) — K + growth hormones + trace minerals
- Langbeinite (0-0-22) — soluble K-Mg-S
- Wood ash (0-1-3) — raises pH, use sparingly

## Compost Tea Brewing

### Veg Tea Recipe (per 5 gallons)
- 1 cup worm castings
- 1 tbsp kelp meal
- 1 tbsp fish hydrolysate
- 1 tbsp unsulfured molasses
- Brew 24-36 hours with aeration

### Flower Tea Recipe (per 5 gallons)
- 1 cup worm castings
- 2 tbsp bat guano (high P)
- 1 tbsp kelp meal
- 1 tbsp molasses
- Brew 24-36 hours with aeration

## Organic IPM (Pest Management)

For a complete monthly IPM calendar, see our [Pest Management Calendar gist](https://gist.github.com/johnhouston707/28fae12b2432bc172cddaa1268c8df02).

### Biological Controls
- *Beauveria bassiana* — fungal pathogen for soft-bodied insects
- *Bacillus thuringiensis* (Bt) — caterpillar/budworm control
- *Trichoderma* species — root zone disease suppression
- Predatory mites (*Amblyseius*, *Phytoseiulus*) — spider mite/thrips control
- *Stratiolaelaps scimitus* — fungus gnat larvae

### Botanical Sprays (Veg Only)
- Neem oil — broad spectrum, stop 2 weeks before flower
- Karanja oil — similar to neem, less phytotoxic
- Pyrethrin (chrysanthemum extract) — knockdown spray, breaks down in UV
