# Matching Cannabis Genetics to Your Growing Environment

## The Framework

The most common mistake growers make is choosing strains based on THC percentage or bag appeal alone. Genetics must match your specific environment to perform optimally.

## Three Key Variables

### 1. Vertical Space

| Ceiling Height | Best Genetics | Stretch Factor |
|---------------|---------------|----------------|
| Under 6 ft | OG Kush, Wedding Cake, Cookies | 1.5-2x |
| 6-8 ft | Gelato, Runtz, GG4, GMO | 2-2.5x |
| 8+ ft | Blue Dream, Jack Herer, Haze | 2.5-3x+ |

### 2. Climate Control Capability

**Poor climate control (fluctuating temps/humidity):**
- Choose mold-resistant genetics with open bud structure
- Best: Durban Poison crosses, Jack Herer, Sour Diesel
- Avoid: Dense indicas (Wedding Cake, ICC, GMO)

**Excellent climate control (stable 75°F, 45-50% RH):**
- Can grow the densest genetics without mold risk
- Best: GMO, Wedding Cake, Ice Cream Cake, Kush Mints

**Hot and dry:**
- Afghan/Kush genetics thrive in arid conditions
- Best: OG Kush, Bubba Kush, Hindu Kush derivatives

### 3. Light Intensity

**Low-medium light (under 800 PPFD):**
- Choose older genetics bred under less intense lights
- Best: Northern Lights, White Widow, Skunk #1
- Modern dessert strains will be airy and disappointing

**High intensity (1000-1500 PPFD):**
- Modern hybrids bred under LED perform best
- Best: Gelato, Runtz, Kush Mints, Wedding Cake

## Quick Setup Guides

### Indoor Tent (4x4, 6-7ft)
- **Best:** OG Kush, Wedding Cake, GMO, Gelato (with training)
- **Avoid:** Haze, Trainwreck, Durban Poison (too stretchy)
- **Training:** Top 2x, LST aggressively, flip at 18 inches

### Indoor Room (8-10ft commercial)
- **Best:** Anything with proper training
- **Top picks:** GMO, Kush Mints, Runtz, Wedding Cake
- **Training:** SCROG or mainlining for even canopy

### Greenhouse (light dep)
- **Best:** Mold-resistant genetics
- **Top picks:** OG Kush, Jack Herer, GG4, Blue Dream
- **Key factor:** Finish time before fall humidity

### Outdoor (full season)
- **Warm climate:** Sativa-dominant (Blue Dream, Durban)
- **Cold/short season:** Indica-dominant (finish before frost)
- **NorCal sweet spots:** OG Kush (finishes early Oct), Blue Dream

## The HLVd Factor

A perfect genetic match still underperforms by 30-50% if carrying Hop Latent Viroid. Always source from PCR-tested nurseries.
